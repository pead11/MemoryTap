How to play

Remember as many sequences as possible!

In Memory Tap, you try to memorize a sequence of flashing buttons and then reproduce it afterwards. To start a round, click the start button at the top of the screen. Then you will see multiple buttons flash purple in a row. Afterwards click the buttons in the same order as the sequence you have seen before, every correctly-clicked button will flash green and the score - which is displayed as a number at the top of the screen - will increase by one. Once you complete a round you can click the startbutton to start the next one, the sequence stays the same but there will be one additional button to memorize at the end of sequence. In the event of an incorrect guess the clicked button will flash red and the game is over. You still have the opportunity to save your score and appear on the leaderboard otherwise you can restart the game.
